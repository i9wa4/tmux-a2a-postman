get-health-oneline — print compact all-session health

Usage:
  tmux-a2a-postman get-health-oneline
  tmux-a2a-postman get-health-oneline --help

Output:
  [0]🔷🟡:🟢 [1]🔴

Marks:
  🔴 stale     pane or session unavailable
  🔷 pending   action-required inbound message is open
  🟡 waiting   outbound reply-required message is open
  🟢 ready     pane is live with no open action or wait

Screen progress fingerprints stay out of this compact view; use get-health
for nodes[*].screen_progress JSON evidence.
